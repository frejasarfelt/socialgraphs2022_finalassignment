this course addresses the question  what organizational form is best suitable for aligning for sustainable societal impact  we begin at the macro level  by addressing global governance mechanism and international agreements  and critically assess voluntary codes of conduct and sector specific initiatives  we unpack collaborative governance by designing and implementing a cross sector partnership  which can be directly applied to the immersive learning projects  in addition to organizational form  we zoom in on organizational culture and on strategic  operational  and tactical organizational layers to analyze the role of the leader in organizational sustainable transformation  we will practice facilitating across different mindsets and value systems  which is a typical hurdle in creating successful cross sector partnerships this course brings in leading scholars in organization science to provide an overview of the different constellations of organizing for sustainability  by working with practical case studies  we apply different organizational solutions in practice the objective of this course is to harness organizational forms that are best positioned to cater to positive societal impact
