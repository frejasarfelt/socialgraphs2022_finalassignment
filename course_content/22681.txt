the course covers a range of subjects in the fields of advanced micro and nanofabrication  areas are defined in collaboration with the students  and the teacher will ensure broadness in the subjects  the course will include training in literature search as well as citation analysis  the specific content of the course changes from semester to semester depending on the teachers and guests participating in the course and on the interests of the participating students  it is expected that the students prepare themselves for each of the presentations by reading background material journal club sessions  presentation of articles the goal of the course is for the students to gain knowledge about topics in advanced micro and nanofabrication and be introduced to critical reading of research literature  furthermore the students will be trained in presentation techniques  accomplished by reading papers presented by the other participants and by presenting papers  a final report on a literature study is prepared and peer reviewed by another course participant and finally handed in at the end of the course
