this course assesses how to design road construction in urban areas based on different road users  needs and behavior and taking into account the environment  traffic planning for   cars  road classes  road types  accessibility  availability  road safety  insecurity  barriers    cyclists   public transport  bus service  traffic  safety and environmental consequences of activities related to noise planning and noise reduction measures traffic driven dimensioning    traffic flows  counts and analyses  delays and queues  capacity assessment of crossings signal control    function and security  geometry  equipment  signal programs  time management  coordination  traffic management lectures  exercises  continuous course assignments  excursions and exercises in the field the aim of the course is to give the student a basic understanding of sustainable traffic planning as well as road technology concepts and techniques for the description and design of road traffic facilities in urban areas digital tools  n2kr tc dankap microstation
