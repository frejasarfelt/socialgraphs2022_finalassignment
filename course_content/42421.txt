the course follows the textbook  managing and organizations  an introduction to theory and practice  by stewart clegg  tyrone pitsis and matt mount  2022  6th edition  with additional material provided by the instructor lectures  case studies and group assignmentsstudents learn to apply concepts  tools and theories related to organizational behavior in their  future  leadership of technology intensive  organizations   the course complements an engineering background and a sensemaking mindset with a hands on understanding of how organizations behave  what is expected of their leaders  and how to accomplish joint objectives  the course includes a discussion of the changing environment in which organizations operate and the strategic capabilities required to manage the changes  a course prepares students to navigate and lead organizational environments
