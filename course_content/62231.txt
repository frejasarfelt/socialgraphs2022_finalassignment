the course provides an introduction to academic theories and concepts in culture  cultural understanding  management  intercultural communication  the importance of language and cultural intelligence  furthermore  an introduction to negotiation  negotiation strategies and the complexity of negotiations is given from an intercultural perspective  the teaching is based on practice  i e  understanding  use and analysis of concrete cases  exercises  experiments  marketing campaigns  negotiations  video conferencing  etc classroom teaching with assignments  discussion and case workthe aim of the course is to enable students to interact purposefully in complex  international and intercultural contexts  e g  intercultural meetings with customers  suppliers  project managers  co workers and management  furthermore  emphasis is placed on enabling students to understand and apply relevant theories in practice  as well as to understand the significance of their own cultural background for intercultural interactions
