this course will give a broad perspective on coatings science and technology  covering the whole life cycle of coatings  topics covered include introduction to coatings  raw materials  coating formulation  coating production  surface preparation  coating application  coating testing and inspection  challenges and outlook in the coatings industry the course will give a broad perspective on coatings    lectures and exercises by the end of this course  it is expected that the students get the basic knowledge on coatings  have the ability to choose more sustainable raw materials and do simple coating formulation for a certain coating application  it is further aimed at getting the students prepared for working in the industry via selected industrial standards and case stories demonstrated in the course and be able to facilitate the provision of more sustainable coating solutions to the industry
