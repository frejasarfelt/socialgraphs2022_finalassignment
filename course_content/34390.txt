the course deals with performance evaluation and control of future telecommunication networks using stochastic modelling and simulation tools  the networks may include mobile and wireless networks  cellular  satellite   high speed networks  internet  wired   wireless   intelligent networks  etc  the actual course content will depend upon and include the most recent problems  networks and tools seminars and group work to describe new directions in telecommunication systems  and introduce performance evaluation methods and resource management at an advanced level master students are allowed to take the course as a special course  the course is also open for participation of other persons than phd students under the open university regulation
