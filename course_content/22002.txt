each year  four new small research projects in the field of audiology will be proposed  first  the students will be familiarized with those projects through some exercises  then the students will be divided into groups and assigned to one of these projects  each group  under the supervision of one phd student  will have to design an experiment  collect some data and analyze the result to test a specific hypothesis related to the project  then they will learn to present their findings to their peers during a poster session lectures and laboratory workthe course aims to provide experience in measurement and analysis methods that are used in audiological clinics and in hearing research  the aim is furthermore to train the participants in oral presentations of scientific material and to produce  present  and discuss a poster the contents of the course will be adapted from year to year to utilize the available measurement equipment
