the overall purpose of the course is to train mmt participants in the service innovation   management concepts at the executive level  this course is designed for both private and public companies within service industries but also companies that want to add service offerings to the business strategy to strengthen the market position  previously more and more industries often failed and were trapped to compete on price because product offerings were commoditized  that forces companies to develop new market strategies  new products services and new understanding of how to organize and finally implement changes the program is based on action learning where theories  methods  concepts and tools are employed on real business challenges and issues from the participants companies  lectures are paralleled by casework  group work and assignments the course objectives are to convey judgment and inspire new thought processes about service and equip the mmt participants with practical use of concepts  frameworks and analytical tools of service innovation   management participation in the program requires separate admission to the mmt program  the price of the mmt program is dkk 380 000  please contact dtu executive mba
