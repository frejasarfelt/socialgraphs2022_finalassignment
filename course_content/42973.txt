the theory of the 4 stage model is presented   route choice calculations are performed using traffic analyst and arcgis for a small test network as preparation for the project work   a road project in the metropolitan area is coded  modelled and evaluated with respect to the traffic impacts  this includes specification of the project  overall need analysis  choice and coding of the alignment  estimating construction costs  calculation of route choice  induced traffic and other traffic impacts  the road project is evaluated by means of a cost benefit analysis lectures and group work with planning of road project  each group defines their own case  in which a road project in the copenhagen region is argued  coded and evaluated the objective of the course is to provide the students with the basic competences within the field of road traffic planning  as well as further develop their arcgis skills to the level that is needed in the road sector the course will be offered for the last time in fall 2023
