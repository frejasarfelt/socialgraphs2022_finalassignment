the course is an experimental project  and the content of the project is strongly related to the ongoing research at dtu health tech  some of the projects will give you the opportunity to work with cell culture  fluorescent microscopy  3d printing  drug delivery formulation  biomaterial testing  biosensor testing and similar fields of research  the project can be considered as a pre msc project independent experimental work for 3 weeks 8 hours a day to introduce students to the experimental techniques used in healthcare engineering the course is mandatory part of msc  eng  advanced materials and healthcare engineering  only students in this programme can register for the course   the course is also offered in august with course number 22206 and in january with course number 22207 students are encouraged to find their own supervisor for the project  eg  the supervisor they want for a future msc project
