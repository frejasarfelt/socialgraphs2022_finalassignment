competences to facilitate innovation in multidisciplinary teams are crucial for future engineers  particularly to thrive in organizations that face global competition and in tackling the societal challenges we face   through the course  students acquires relevant experiences and competences with a systematic approach to facilitating innovation in an engineering context  students will develop unique insights into how the facilitation of integrated innovation is practiced  they will experience multidisciplinary collaboration through facilitating a multidisciplinary team in doing an innovation project on a chosen innovation theme  each project team  consisting of students that are taking the  sister course  42504 innovation in engineering  will be assigned a team facilitator  as a facilitator you will bring your advanced innovation competences to the table and guide a multidisciplinary team during all project work   the course is organized in two main parts  the first week   called the boost module   is focused on engaging the students with the topic of innovation  and introducing students to a systematic approach to facilitating innovation  as well as other relevant theory  during the first week  students will also form teams around a chosen innovation theme  the second and third week   called the experience module   are focused on facilitating a team in doing an innovation project    the course consists of online lectures  reading material  group exercises games and assignments  peer reviews and multiple choice quizzes  a large part of the learning activities are group based and it thus crucial to participate in class the course is a mix of lectures  video lectures  exercises and project work  part will run as an online course  with group work taking place on campus the course aims to equip students with key competences regarding facilitation of innovation in multidisciplinary teams in an engineering context  students will    understand and experience what it takes to facilitate innovation     see the big picture of the innovation landscape  and find their position and role given their advanced innovation and facilitation competences     lead and contribute to innovation initiatives in team context   acquire a signature dtu mindset and further develop their approach to innovation    be able to communicate to relevant stakeholders   know about a toolkit of methods and tools for facilitating innovationthis course is connected and runs in parallel to the  sister course   42504 innovation in engineering  the sister course  42504  is intended
