the course consists of a number of introductory lectures  assignments based on the lectures and a general assignment based on the course prerequisites  contents of the course are mechatronic design of unmanned systems and their mechanical modeling  control  perception  navigation  software  simulation  experimentation with unmanned aerial vehicles  the final task is solved by using one of the aut unmanned aerial vehicles laboratory assignment  complemented by lectures and exercises to give the students a hands on experience into the development  integration  test and validation of the most relevant theories for autonomous systems
