the course treats new methods in the areas of image and video coding  the topics covered are  new methods for optimization of entropy coding and estimation of entropy in image data  wavelet and multi resolution coding of multi dimensional image data  interplay between communication networks and image video coding  advanced video coding  video quality  distributed coding  multi camera and multiview coding as well as coding of graphics  drone image and video communication is considered as example  the specific content is dependent on the newest problems and current projects seminars  group work and project the aim is to introduce new trends in the areas of image and video coding and communication this course is suitable as part of a ph d  study plan
