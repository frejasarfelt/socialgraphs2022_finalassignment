1  measuring uncertainty and traceability  2  geometrical product specifications  3  coordinate metrology  measurements on cmms   4  digitalisation and reverse engineering  5  surface metrology  6  optical metrology  7  tolerance verification  8  machine testing the course is taught over 13 days  covering different topics  each day is devoted to a single topic  to give a complete coverage of the topic  on the basis of lectures  demonstrations    the course is focused on providing a structured vision of geometrical metrology as the decisional basis for controlling design  manufacture  and function  and a basic discipline  which can serve as powerful tool to solve industrial  as well as scientific problems  the course encompasses a number of hands on activities concerning the use of measurement instruments  data processing and analyses of industrial problems  the quality assurance of measurements is emphasized in the context of the metrological infrastructure at company  national  and international level
