the course consists of a series of lectures on vehicle dynamics and  corresponding hand in assignments  in addition  a larger project work related to vehicles  mechanics of the wheel  tire and wheel  suspension and springs  stability and steering  braking and distribution of brake forces  forces and distribution of these  driving resistance  rise and acceleration is carried out  the students also have to reflect on the material selection and possibly the production methods  the project work is documented in a report project work in groups and lectures with hand in exercisesto give the students knowledge of the basic conditions concerning vehicle properties  to perform a larger project related to components of a vehicle  including specification of the requirements  dimensioning  and selection of material and production method  the project is based on specific design challenges from the dtu blue dot projects related to vehicles as e g  roadrunners  formula student or the wind car  the students should supply specific and well argued solutions to the challenges
