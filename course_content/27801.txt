the following topics will be addressed     good and bad supervision    the supervisor task  project or process    clarification of mutual expectations    learning objectives    motivation   reflections on own supervision practice    the examiner and censor role   grading by the 7 step scalediscussions  logbook  self assessments  and reflection exercises the aim of the course is to make the students reflect upon the supervisor role  and provide them with tools useful for their supervision practices  by sharing knowledge about their own experiences  the students will acquire a basic understanding of the challenges of being a supervisor and establish an idea of how to be  a good supervisor   during the course  the students will gain some of the necessary generic skills for developing their supervisor competencies
