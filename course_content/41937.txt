the course mainly consists of lectures and project work in groups with sub assignments  in the project group  the students design a multi storey precast concrete building based on the topics from the lectures and most often in accordance with the european standards  some of the methods learned are also applicable for buildings of other materials   the students are graded based on the assessment of one or two reports about topics from the project work  the students will get formative feedback during the course  e g  via peer assessment  some of the topics are  load combinations  design of horizontal and vertical members  stabilizing walls  the rigid floor method  structural joints etc lectures and projectwork in groupsthe main objective is for the students to be able to carry out a structural design of a multi storey building consisting of mainly precast concrete elements
